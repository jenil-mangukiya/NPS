Sports:

With an aim to grow a Winning character among the students, NPS leverages various Sports. From the plethora of values that sports infuse into a child, we believe

that the most important are - Discipline, Passion, Team Spirit and Dedication.

We also believe that every child should experience the thrill of adventure. Hence we arrange adventure camps during school holidays. So we promote exciting activities like trekking, skiing, rock climbing, river rafting, river crossing, bridge swinging, rappelling etc. The school offers facilities for many Indoor- Outdoor games.
